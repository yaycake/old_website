Discover Places To Eat

Yaochima is a WeChat Mini Program that helps you decide on where to eat next with a few quick shakes of your phone.

Local Chinese apps like Dianping or Meituan overwhelm users with endless and overwhelming possibilities of where to eat. In the restaurant-riddled city of Chengdu, the freedom to judge all your options arrests you and your friends in a paradox of choice.

With each shake of your phone, Yaochima shows you nearby restaurants within walking distance. Each result displays cuisine and price range: if you donâ€™t lock either factor and shake again, those categories are filtered out of future results to help you narrow down and make a decision.

To localize and enthuse the use of the app by locals of Chengdu, we recorded, edited, and added Sichuan exclamations and randomized them to crow with each shake.

All icons, logos, and graphics were created and coded by me.
